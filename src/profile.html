4TH USG SENATE REGULAR SESSION

The USG Senate

convened for its fourth session, held at Meeting Room, Hinang Hall, promptly commencing at 2:00 in the afternoon and was presided over by USG Senate President Juniel Rey B. Gigaquit. The session adhered strictly to parliamentary protocol, initiating with a prayer and roll call to establish attendance. This session served as a vital practice simulation for the forthcoming 1st Reading of Bills, meticulously guided by Resource Speakers Louie Joash A. Dela Torre and Christian Kylle A. Balase. Notably, the session focused on the scrutiny and refinement of bills, setting for subsequent readings and revisions. The diligent deliberations pave the way for thorough examination and constructive amendments, demonstrating the Senate's commitment to effective governance and legislative excellence. #CSUMainUSG
